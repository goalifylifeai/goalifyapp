Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 11, 2025  ·  Last updated: May 11, 2025

Goalify ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal growth app that helps you set goals, build habits, and reflect on your life. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using Goalify, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

  • Email address — used to create and identify your account.
  • Password — stored securely as a hash; we never see your plain-text password.
  • If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive only your email address and a unique account identifier from that provider. We do not receive your social media profile, contacts, or any other data from those providers.

Profile information (provided by you during setup)

  • Display name — the name used in your daily check-ins and coaching messages.
  • Pronouns (optional) — used to personalise coaching language. You can skip this entirely.
  • Life focus areas — which of the four spheres (finance, health, career, relationships) matter most to you.
  • Coaching tone preference — warm, direct, or playful.

Content you create

  • Goals and subtasks
  • Daily habits and streak data
  • Journal entries
  • Daily intentions and ritual check-ins
  • Messages sent to the AI Coach
  • Future self letter

All of this content is private to you and is never shared with other users or third parties.

Calendar data

If you enable the "Add to Calendar" feature for a habit, Goalify requests permission to write a recurring event to your device's calendar. We only create events you explicitly ask for. We do not read your existing calendar events.

Notification preferences

If you enable push notifications, we store your preferred notification times (morning, lunch, evening) on your device. Notifications are scheduled locally — we do not use a third-party push service to deliver reminder notifications.

Technical data

We may collect anonymous crash reports and basic usage diagnostics (e.g. app version, device operating system) to help us fix bugs. This data contains no personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account.
  • To provide the features of the app — goals, habits, coaching, journal, rituals.
  • To personalise the AI Coach's tone and language to your preferences.
  • To send you opt-in local notifications for morning, lunch, and evening check-ins.
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues.

We do not use your data to show you advertisements. We do not sell your data. Ever.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your account data and app content are stored on servers provided by Supabase, a third-party backend service. All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption). Supabase's servers are located in the European Union (Frankfurt, Germany) by default.

Supabase processes data on our behalf and is contractually bound not to use your data for any purpose other than providing the database service. You can review Supabase's own privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy.

4. Third-Party Sign-In

You may sign in using Google or Apple. In these cases, the respective provider authenticates you and shares only your email address and a unique identifier with us. We do not receive any other data from your Google or Apple account. Please review Google's and Apple's own privacy policies for information on how they handle your data during sign-in.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data — goals, habits, journal entries, coaching history, profile, and future letter — is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Export a copy of your data.
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@goalify.life. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Goalify is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the European Union). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us at privacy@goalify.life and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If changes are significant, we will notify you in the app. Continued use of Goalify after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
