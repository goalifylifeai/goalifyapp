## Full Description (max 4000 characters)

```
Goalify is your personal growth companion — a calm, focused space to set meaningful goals, build daily habits, and reflect on what matters most.

Most productivity apps make you feel like you're always behind. Goalify is different. It works around four areas of your life — finance, health, career, and relationships — and helps you make steady, intentional progress without the noise.

──────────────────────────
WHAT YOU CAN DO
──────────────────────────

▸ Set Goals with milestones
  Break big ambitions into subtasks. Track progress visually across your life's most important areas.

▸ Build Daily Habits
  Create habits that repeat, build streaks, and optionally sync to your calendar so you never lose track.

▸ Morning & Evening Rituals
  Start and end each day with a short check-in. Set your daily intention in the morning. Reflect on what went well in the evening.

▸ Private Journal
  Write freely. Your entries stay private, and Goalify uses sentiment tracking to help you notice patterns in how you feel over time.

▸ AI Coach
  Get insights, weekly reflections, and thoughtful nudges from a coach that knows your goals, habits, and life areas — and adapts to your preferred coaching style.

▸ Future Self
  Write a letter to your future self. Come back to it as a reminder of where you're headed and why.

──────────────────────────
PERSONALISED FROM THE START
──────────────────────────

Goalify adapts to you. During setup, choose:
  • Which life areas matter most right now
  • Your preferred coaching tone — warm, direct, or playful
  • Your pronouns, for gender-aware coaching language

──────────────────────────
YOUR DATA STAYS YOURS
──────────────────────────

Goalify does not sell your data. Your goals, journal entries, and habits are stored securely and are never shared with advertisers or third parties. You can request deletion of your account and all data at any time.

──────────────────────────
NO ADS. NO CLUTTER.
──────────────────────────

Just you, your goals, and a coach that's always in your corner.

Download Goalify and start building the life you actually want.
```
